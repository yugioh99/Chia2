FTLE#

FTLE: Finite Time Lyapunov Exponent (FTLE) is a measure of the rate of stretching of fluid parcels within a time-varying velocity field over a finite time interval, used to understand and visualize transport mechanisms in unsteady flows. (key fi)

In STS, FTLE is used to search for LCS.

LCS#

LCS: Lagrangian Coherent Structures (LCS) are like "material lines of attraction and repulsion" that help us understand how fluid particles move and mix over time, acting as pathways or barriers for fluid transport. In STS, LCS are used to speculate on the fluid dynamics of market sentiment. (key la)

Pitchfork#

Pitchforks: also called median lines, are drawing tools. In STS, pitchforks are used to search for the FTLE of LCS in market sentiment. (key pi)
